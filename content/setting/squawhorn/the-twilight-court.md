The Twilight Court is the seat of the high council of Squawhorn, the nation from which [Aelyra Caladwen](../../../characters/main-characters/aelyra-caladwen) and [Syren Ashryver](../../../characters/main-characters/syren-ashryver) hail. It is ruled by [Emporer Caladwen](../../../characters/npcs/humanoid/emporer-caladwen).
