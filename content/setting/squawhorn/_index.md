Squawhorn is a neighboring country to [Estharia](../../estharia/). It is a wealthy, war-waging nation and home to The Twilight Court. [Syren Ashriver](../../../main-characters/syren-ashryver and [Aelyra Caladwen](../../../main-characters/aelyra-caladwen come from this nation.
