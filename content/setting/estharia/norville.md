Norville is a mid-sized trading town where travelers frequently come and go. Adventurers looking for a place to rest, merchants looking to trade, and craftspeople looking for short-term work frequently come and go.

## The Lute & Lyre

The Lute & Lyre is a quiet tavern located in Norville. It's run by an angel of a woman, similar to Grandma Rocky, and is frequented by day drunks and bar flies. It's notable for having a large stage for performances, which are put to use somewhat regularly and without much schedule.

## Findlay's Lamp Emporium

[Findlay's](../../../characters/npcs/humanoid/findlay) Lamp Emporium is a tall, narrow, Weasley-house-esque building that is exactly what it sounds like. Findlay and his imp servants produce all manner of lamps and lamp-like objects, some of which are magical in nature. The Lamp Emporium is host to a direct portal to The Abyss, the demonic realm from whence the imps and demons came. A magical welcome mat embued with the words "Not Welcome" keeps the demons from coming into the material plane from The Abyss.

In the first session of the game, the lamp emporium is the focal point of the story. Findlay had enslaved a demon and several imps to do his bidding in the lamp shop, both personally and professionally, but he accidentally spilled tea on his demonic contracts, freeing them from their magical bindings, and they quickly overthrew Findlay and removed him from power. The player characters ultimately decided to free the imps from both Findlay's control as well as [Belvedere's](../../../characters/npcs/demons/belvedere).
