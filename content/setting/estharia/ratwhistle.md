Ratwhistle is the hometown of [Vesper Treadway](../../../characters/main-characters/vesper-treadway). It's a small, quaint village, mostly inhabited by farmers who grow and tend to the farmlands, and a small band of thieves who look out for each other. They steal from other towns and bring the spoils back to Ratwhistle to share with the residents, including those who do not partake in thievery.
