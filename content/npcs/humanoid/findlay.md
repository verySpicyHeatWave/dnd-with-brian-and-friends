Findlay is the owner of the Lamp Emporium in [Norville](../../../../setting/estharia/norville). He had enslaved the demon [Belvedere](../../demons/belvedere) as his personal servent as well as several [Imps](../../imps/) as his workers, until he accidentally destroyed his demonic contracts in [Session #1](../../../../story/s001-the-lamp-shop), freeing the demon and imps from their bonds. The players freed the imps and implored Findlay to hire paid workers in the future instead of meddling with the enslavement of demons and imps.
