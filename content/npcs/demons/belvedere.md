Belvedere was [Findlay's](../../humanoid/findlay) personal servant, enslaved by a demonic contract. He was freed in Session #1 by Findlay after he had accidentally spilled tea on the demonic contracts, but then took power over the imps personally. He was killed by the player characters at the end of the session, freeing the imps and allowing them to return home to The Abyss.
